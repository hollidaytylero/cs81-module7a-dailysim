# Reflection – Daily Schedule Simulator

## What was your approach to designing the schedule?
I just chose the acitivies I repeated the most this summer since I have had a pretty regular schedule.

## What was one challenge or unexpected behavior you encountered?
I didnt know how to make the animation stop at the end. So, I had to try a few different things to get it to work.

## What does this assignment teach you about async code?
This assignment showed me how async code makes events happen over time instead of all at once. This allows the schedule to update overtime rather than all at once. 

## What creative element did you add?
I added surprise events (different ones) and a random mood at the begininning of the day.

## How does this project simulate or differ from real-world schedules?
It's different from real-world schedules because real-world schedules are dynamic and change everyday. So, this project does not simulate that.
